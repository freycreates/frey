FREY. behind the decks

Pop-culture enthusiast raised on Gaga, Britney and ABBA, finding religion in the queer club scene. In my sets, discover your own church through pop girls from today and yesterday.

Since 2022, I have been a regular at queer clubs and festivals across the Netherlands, Brussels and Singapore. Venues and festivals I’ve played include Club NYX, FERRY, Paradiso, Homomonument, Milkshake Festival and Hullabaloo.
